How it works

Simple, warm,
relationship-first

Four steps from sign-up to a companion sitting with your elder over a cup of tea.

01
📋
You register
Tell us about your elder — their interests, language, personality, and what kind of companionship they need.
02
🤝
We match
We hand-pick a companion based on shared interests, language, temperament, and location. Not an algorithm — a human decision.
03
📱
Free intro call
First session is always a free 30-min video call. No home visit until your elder feels fully comfortable.
04
🌿
Sessions begin
Regular visits, calls, or outings. Family gets a WhatsApp update every session. The relationship deepens over time.

Safety & trust

Every companion is
thoroughly vetted

We know you're inviting someone into your parent's home. We take that seriously.

🪪
Aadhaar verified
Every companion's identity is verified with Aadhaar before their first session.
🔍
Police verified
Background check completed before any home visit is permitted.
📚
Trained & certified
4-module training on elder empathy, active listening, safety, and health observation.
📍
GPS check-in
Family gets a WhatsApp notification the moment the companion arrives and leaves.

No cash transactions
All payments through the platform only. No money changes hands during sessions.

Session notes
